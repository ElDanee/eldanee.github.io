About Me

Hi, I'm Daniele Alessi.
I'm an Italian student, currently completing my master degree in Computer Science & Engineering at Politecnico di Milano.

I'm studying computer graphics, GPU programming and parallel computing, with a particular focus on Vulkan both as graphics API and GPGPU platform.
I'm also learning WebGPU, and I'm proficient in GLSL.
